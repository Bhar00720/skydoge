How to Play

1

Move

Use A/D or ←/→ to dodge obstacles. Use W/↑ to boost and S/↓ to slow.

2

Dodge Everything

Red bars, amber orbs, purple walls, and homing missiles will try to end your run.

3

Collect Powerups

🟢 Score Orb (+25pts) · 🔵 Shield (1 free hit) · 🟡 Slow-Mo (3s) · 🟣 Magnet (attracts orbs)

4

Near Misses

Narrowly dodge obstacles for +5 bonus points and a satisfying zing!

5

Boss Encounters

Every 500 points, face a massive obstacle pattern that requires precise dodging.

Controls

A/← Move Left · D/→ Move Right
W/↑ Boost · S/↓ Slow
Space Activate Powerup · P/Esc Pause
R Restart · M Main Menu
